About Us

At Suyay Tour, we're not a big agency. We're a small group of people who love Peru and want to share it with you in a personal, authentic way.

Our name, Suyay, means "hope" in Quechua — and that's exactly what we want to give you: an authentic, relaxed experience filled with unforgettable moments. From airport pickup to your last stroll through our streets, you’ll feel like part of our family.

We offer city tours in Lima, trips to Huacachina, food tours, and more. But most importantly, we offer a local perspective — warm, patient, and full of passion to help you fall in love with Peru just like we did.

You're invited to experience it with us. Welcome to Suyay Tour.
