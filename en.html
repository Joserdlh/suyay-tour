About Us

Suyay Tour is not a big agency. We are a group of people who love Peru and want to share it with you in a close and authentic way.

Our name, Suyay, means "hope" in Quechua — and that's what we want to offer: an authentic, peaceful experience full of memorable moments. From the airport pickup to the last walk through our streets, you'll be treated like family.

We organize city tours in Lima, trips to Huacachina, gastronomic routes and more — but most importantly, we offer a local perspective with care, patience, and a deep love for Peru.

You’re invited to live it with us. Welcome to Suyay Tour.
