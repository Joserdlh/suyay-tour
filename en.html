About Us

Suyay Tour is not a big agency. We're a group of people who love Peru and want to share it with you in a close and authentic way.

Our name, Suyay, means "hope" in Quechua — and that’s what we aim to give: a genuine, peaceful experience full of memorable moments. From the moment we pick you up at the airport to the last stroll through our streets, you'll feel like part of the family.

We offer city tours in Lima, trips to Huacachina, culinary routes and more, but most of all, we offer a local view, with love, patience, and a desire for you to fall in love with Peru as we have.

You're invited to live it with us. Welcome to Suyay Tour.
